Best Episode(s) for Each "New Who" Doctor:


The 9th Doctor

"Dalek" (S1:E6)

We begin to really hear about the mysterious Time War, and an ancient foe is resurrected.


The 10th Doctor

"The Girl in the Fireplace" (S2:E4)

A one-off episode that brings out 10's humanity and the heartbreak at leaving people behind when you travel through time. Cute twist at the very end


"Blink" (S3:E10)

The Doctor is barely in this one, but we meet a deadly new foe. Masterful stroytelling and a brief nod to Back to the Future.


"Silence in the Library / Forest of the Dead" (S4:E8-9)

The Doctor meets a mysterious person from his future.


The 11th Doctor

"Vincent and the Doctor" (S5:E10)

When the Doctor discovers something nefarious in a Van Gogh painting, he just has to investigate. If you are an artist of any kind, this one will make you cry.


"A Good Man Goes to War" (S6:E7)

"Demons run when a good man goes to war." Enough said.


"The Angels Take Manhattan" (S7:E5)

Paradoxes and heartbreak and inevitabilities abound!


The 12th Doctor

"Deep Breath" (S8:E1)

The 12th Doctor makes his first appearance and is really having a hard time handling his new self and his attack eyebrows.


"Under the Lake/Before the Flood" (S9:E3-4)

A classic time travel riddle played out in a quintessentially "Doctor Who" way.
